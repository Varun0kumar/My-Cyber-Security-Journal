# Commands Used

## Enable Logging

auditpol /set /category:"Logon/Logoff" /success:enable /failure:enable
auditpol /set /category:"Account Logon" /success:enable /failure:enable
auditpol /set /category:"Detailed Tracking" /success:enable

## Failed Log Extraction

Get-WinEvent -FilterHashtable @{LogName='Security'; ID=4625} -MaxEvents 30 |
Select-Object TimeCreated, Id,
@{Name='User';Expression={$*.Properties[5].Value}},
@{Name='SourceIP';Expression={$*.Properties[19].Value}},
@{Name='LogonType';Expression={$_.Properties[8].Value}} |
Out-File C:\Users\Administrator\failed_logs.txt

## Successful Log Extraction

Get-WinEvent -FilterHashtable @{LogName='Security'; ID=4624} -MaxEvents 30 |
Select-Object TimeCreated, Id,
@{Name='User';Expression={$*.Properties[5].Value}},
@{Name='SourceIP';Expression={$*.Properties[18].Value}},
@{Name='LogonType';Expression={$_.Properties[8].Value}} |
Out-File C:\Users\Administrator\successful_logs.txt

## Process Execution Logs

Get-WinEvent -FilterHashtable @{LogName='Security'; ID=4688} -MaxEvents 30 |
Select-Object TimeCreated,
@{Name='ProcessName';Expression={$*.Properties[5].Value}},
@{Name='CommandLine';Expression={$*.Properties[8].Value}},
@{Name='User';Expression={$_.Properties[1].Value}} |
Out-File C:\Users\Administrator\process_logs.txt
